Privacy Policy for Baby Sleep Trainer

Last updated: 18 September 2025

Thank you for choosing Baby Sleep Trainer. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Our app is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We collect only the minimum information necessary for the app to function.

  • Microphone Access: The core function of our app requires access to your device's microphone to monitor sound levels. The audio data is processed in real-time, directly on your device. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio from your microphone.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect any personal data, analytics, or usage statistics. Your settings and interactions are confined to your device.

How We Use Your Information

Because we do not collect any personal information, we do not use it for any purpose. The microphone data is used solely for the immediate functionality of detecting sound levels and is immediately discarded.

Data Sharing

We do not collect any data, so there is no data to share. We will never share, sell, or rent any information with third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. We advise you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
